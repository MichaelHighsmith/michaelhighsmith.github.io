LifeUp

LifeUp was Satyr Labs first project to be published on the Google Play Store. This productivity app allows users to create a To-Do list that gives them gold and experience for completing tasks, while taking away gold when engaging in unproductive behaviors. After accruing enough gold users are able to purchase accessories for their character and engage in battle with enemies!

MemeHub

MemeHub is an image sharing site where users can both submit their own images and swipe left and right to vote on other user's content. As you swipe your newsfeed refines itself to only display the type of images you prefer to see. If you only want images of puppies, images of puppies will be all that you see!

NewsNet

NewsNet is a news app that concentrates all of your favorite news sources into one application, thereby increasing your efficiency in keeping up with today's current events. From ABC News to ESPN to TechCrunch, NewsNet allows users to keep tabs on events happening all around the globe!
